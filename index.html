Research Interests

My research focuses on evaluating the reliability and robustness of LLMs in clinical and agentic workflows. I use geometric diagnostics, structured perturbations (e.g., masking, negation), and latent boundary analysis to surface silent failures. I'm also interested in low-rank approximation techniques like PCA to study boundary flip rates, and building interpretable agent-based systems.
